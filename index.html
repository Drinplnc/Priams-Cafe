About us

As a small coffee shop, In the current state of our business, the winds of change have brought challenges. There are days when the footfall dwindles to just 1-2 customers. Nevertheless, Priam's Cafe is not just surviving; we're doing good. The warmth of our patrons' love has been a constant beacon, with many showing their affection for Priam's Cafe. This not only fuels our passion but reinforces our commitment to continue brewing and serving, knowing that our community stands firmly by our side. Thank you, kapetbahays!

History

Owners: Leonor Bianca Lacostales and Jerel Lacostales

Hello everyone. I’m Bianca Lacostales, one of the co-owners of Priam’s Cafe. Let’s embark on a journey into the heart of Priam's Cafe, where my personal love story with coffee began back in 2022. Beyond being a business, Priam's stands as a warm haven, a cozy space meticulously crafted alongside my husband. Together, hand in hand, we've curated every recipe, pouring our shared love for exceptional coffee into each cup.
